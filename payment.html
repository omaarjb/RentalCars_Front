Payment Methods

Cash

We accept payment with cash. Please note that we require a deposit for the rental, which can be paid in cash at the time of pickup. Any remaining balance must also be paid in cash before returning the rental.

Mastercard

We accept Mastercard payments through our secure payment gateway service. You can make a payment online using your Mastercard by clicking on the "Pay with Mastercard" button and following the instructions on the checkout page.

PayPal

We accept PayPal payments for car rental. You can make a payment securely online using your PayPal account by clicking on the "Pay with PayPal" button and following the instructions on the checkout page.

Check

We accept payment by check. To make a payment by check, please make the check payable to [Your Company Name] and mail it to [Your Company Address]. Please note that we require a deposit for the rental, which must be paid by check and received before the rental pickup date. Any remaining balance must also be paid by check before returning the rental.
