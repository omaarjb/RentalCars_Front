FAQs

Q: How old do I need to be to rent a car?

A: The minimum age requirement is 21 years old. However, some vehicle categories may require a minimum age of 25. Please check our terms and conditions for more information.

Q: Can I rent a car with a debit card?

A: Yes, we accept debit cards as a form of payment. Please note that a deposit may be required, and additional verification may be needed.

Q: What documents do I need to rent a car?

A: You will need a valid driver's license, a credit or debit card, and a proof of insurance (if you decline our insurance coverage).
